Our Story

CV. Global Nest has been providing high quality swiflet bird's nest since 1993. Located in Yogyakarta, Indonesia, the family business has expanded and we have changed our business model to ensure the quality and satisfaction of our customers around the world. From our bird's nest farm to our customers, we have the ability to control the supply chain therefore we are able to manage the quality and nutrition of our products.

In 1993, we shipped our first products to the United States and have been granted FDA Certification. In 1995, we began to export to China before expanding our wings to other countries. We always provide high quality products and reliable services to build long-term business with our customers or wholesalers. Today, we export to China, the United States, Canada, Taiwan, Hong Kong, Singapore, Australia and Malaysia.
